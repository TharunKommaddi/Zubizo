Our Story

Where Art Meets Celebration

Zubizo is more than a brand—it's a celebration of love, culture, and exquisite craftsmanship. As a passionate entrepreneur, I've built Zubizo to transform your most precious moments into timeless works of art.

Every invitation we create is a masterpiece, meticulously designed with cultural authenticity and contemporary elegance. From stunning temple illustrations to delicate calligraphy, from luxurious wax seals to shimmering foil accents— we craft invitations that tell your unique love story.

With worldwide shipping and an unwavering commitment to perfection, we bring our artistry to celebrations across the globe. Based in Trichy, Tamil Nadu, we serve couples everywhere who demand nothing but the finest for their special day.

Project Details

The Royal Purple Collection represents the pinnacle of luxury wedding stationery. This stunning suite features rich purple cardstock with gold foil stamping, hand-poured wax seals, and intricate laser-cut details. Each invitation is embellished with traditional motifs and modern calligraphy, creating a perfect balance between heritage and contemporary design. The collection includes the main invitation, RSVP cards, thank you notes, and coordinating envelopes—all packaged in a custom presentation box.

The Botanical Elegance collection brings the beauty of nature to your wedding stationery.
